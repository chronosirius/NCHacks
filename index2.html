What our website is about:

We created this website for the NorCalHacks Competition. We coded a website which gives you easy access to information about land and sea animals in the form of flip cards. We chose this as our project as thinking about the theme, we thought land and sea animals fit the topic perfectly.

Sharks

Sharks are a group of predatory fish known for their distinctive appearance and powerful swimming abilities. There are over 400 species of sharks, ranging in size from the small lantern shark to the large whale shark. Sharks have a strong sense of smell.

Killer Whales

Killer whales, also known as orcas, are the largest dolphins and are apex predators found in oceans all over the world. They are highly intelligent and social animals and live in family groups called pods. Orcas are apex predators and feed on a variety of prey, including fish, seals, and whales.

Seal

Seals are aquatic mammals known for their sleek bodies, flippers, and the ability to live both in the water and on land. There are 33 species of seals, including the harbor seal, elephant seal, and leopard seal. Seals feed on a variety of prey, including fish, squid, and krill.

Octopus

Octopuses are intelligent and highly adaptable creatures belonging to the mollusk family. They have soft, flexible bodies and are known for their ability to change color and texture to blend in with their surroundings.

Jellyfish

Jellyfish are predatory creatures belonging to the phylum Cnidaria. They are known for their transparent, bell-shaped bodies and long, trailing tentacles. Jellyfish are found in oceans all over the world, from warm, shallow waters to the deep sea.

Dolphin

Dolphins are highly intelligent marine mammals belonging to the family Delphinidae. They are found in oceans and seas all over the world and are known for their playful and social behavior. Dolphins have a streamlined body and a powerful tail fin, or fluke, that propels them.
